The above links provide indexes only. If you would like to have the articles or back issues themselves, you will need to contact the publisher.

FMR was a bi-monthly magazine dedicated to art. It is of Italian origin and named after its creator and first and long time editor Franco Maria Ricci, but also appearing in English (the edition which I have and is indexed above). It was taken over by the FMR spa/ART'E' group, so logically:-) after 127 issues it started again with issue 1 in June 2004. It produced another 33 issues and maybe another 10 so-called white issues and then ceased to exist in 2009. (It seems that I still have to update the index to #33 and the FMR White's:-) The listed web site does not seem to have any reference any more to the journal FMR.
